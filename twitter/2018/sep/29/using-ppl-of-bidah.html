Using The People Of Bidah

The great scholar Ibn Baz [رحمه الله] said:

«للأسف أن أعداء الإسلام يستعينون بمن ينتسبون إلى الإسلام من الخرافيين والصوفيين وسائر أهل البدع ليروجوا باطلهم.»

❝It is regretful that the enemies of Islaam seek aid with those who ascribe themselves to Islaam from those who believe in myths and superstitions, and also the Soofeeyah and all the rest of the people of Bida so they can promote their falsehood.❞

📚 [Al-Fatawa, (3/130) | Translated By Abbas Abu Yahya Miraath al-Anbiyya]
